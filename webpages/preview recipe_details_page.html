Spicy Miso Ramen

Japanese • 25 min • Medium • by @noodlelover
🍜

Description

A rich and comforting bowl of ramen with a deep umami-packed miso broth, gentle heat from chili, and perfectly chewy noodles. Finished with a soft egg and fresh toppings, this dish is both cozy and bold — perfect for a chilly evening.

🌶️ Spicy 🍜 Comfort ⏱️ Quick meal

Ingredients

Steps

1
Boil noodles according to instructions until perfectly tender.
2
Prepare broth by mixing miso and chili paste into hot stock.
3
Add noodles to broth and simmer briefly to absorb flavor.
4
Serve hot with egg and your favorite toppings.
